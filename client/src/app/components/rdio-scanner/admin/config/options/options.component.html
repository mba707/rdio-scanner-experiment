AGC Makeup Gain
Boost in decibels applied after compression when the AGC toggle is on. Higher values raise quiet transmissions more, but also raise background noise between words.

AGC makeup gain is required AGC makeup gain must be between 0 and 40 dB

AGC Ratio
Compression ratio of the AGC. Higher values flatten loud and quiet transmissions closer together.

AGC ratio is required AGC ratio must be between 1 and 20

AGC Threshold
Level in decibels above which the AGC starts compressing. Lower (more negative) values compress more of the audio.

AGC threshold is required AGC threshold must be between -100 and 0 dB

Quality factor of the notch filter. Lower values widen the notch (catch more around the center frequency but affect voice more); higher values narrow it.

Notch Q is required Notch Q must be between 0.1 and 100
